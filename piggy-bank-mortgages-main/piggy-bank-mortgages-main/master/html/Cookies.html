Cookies...


Cookies are files saved on your phone, tablet or computer when you visit a website.

We use cookies to store information about how you use this website.


Cookie settings

We use 2 types of cookies. You can choose what you are happy for us to use.


Cookies that measure website use

We use Google Analytics to measure how you use the website, website performance, and your experience while visiting so we can continually make improvements. We do not allow Google to use or share the data about how you use this site.


Google Analytics sets cookies that store anonymised information about:

  • How you got to the website
  • The pages you visit on the website and how long you spent on a page
  • What you click on the website while visiting
