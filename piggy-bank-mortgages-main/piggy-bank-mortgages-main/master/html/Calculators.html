How long will you need to save for?

The average desposit in the UK is £45,000 outside London and £80,000 in London.

Usually you will need between 5% and 20% of the price of a property as a deposit.

For example, to buy a £200,000 house you will need between £10,000 and £40,000 as a deposit.

Information source: Money Advice Service

On average it takes between 6 and 8 years to save for a deposit.

This varies depending on location in the UK.

In London for example, it can take first time buyers up to 11 years to reach their deposit goal.

Information source: Financial Reporter
